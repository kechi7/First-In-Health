First In Health

A resource hub for first-generation students, marginaized identities, professionals, and their allies. Through information, guidance, and support we can help mitigate the unique challenges faced by first-gen individuals and minorities embarking on educational and career journies in healthcare. By fostering a better understanding of the barriers faced by marginalized identities in healthcare, we will be able to help rectify the inequalities of our healthcare system today.
